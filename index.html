How it works

LeakAI combines IoT, cloud data management, and AI to streamline water leak detection.

Data from field devices like Leak Locator is recorded in real-time, analysed using machine learning to spot trends, pinpoint leaks, and even nullify daytime noise. Ground listening is enhanced by sound amplification and data upload to LeakAI Cloud for precise leak localisation.

LeakAI Locator and LeakAI Cloud automate the previously time-consuming process. Compatibility with various noise recording devices offers flexibility.
