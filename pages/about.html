Our Revolutionary Road Map to Transforming Leak Detection in Water Networks

  1. Development of LeakAI Cloud: Creation of a scalable spatial data management solution, implemented in numerous projects, with enhanced data security. Provides software as a service (SaaS) for real-time data recording, offline data storage, and data analysis using machine learning algorithms.
  2. Development of LeakAI Locator: Integration of an acoustic recorder and correlator with a state-of-the-art microcontroller (ESP32). Enables automated data collection and correlation, eliminating the need for manual placement and retrieval of acoustic recorders.
  3. Integration with third-party leak devices: Compatibility with various noise recording devices, allowing users to choose the solution that best suits their needs. Provides flexibility and ease of adoption for existing leak detection systems.
  4. International Expansion: Plans to expand globally, targeting water networks in different countries and regions. Aims to establish partnerships and penetrate new markets to bring the benefits of LeakAI technology to a wider customer base.
  5. Expansion of LeakAI to other sectors: Diversification beyond water networks to address additional sectors such as wastewater, oil, and gas leak detection. Leverages the capabilities of LeakAI Cloud and Locator to offer innovative solutions in multiple industries.
